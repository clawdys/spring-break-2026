📍 Neighborhood Tips

Jordaan — Cozy canals, indie shops, brown cafés. Best for wandering.
De Pijp — Albert Cuyp Market, multicultural food, lively bars.
Oud-West — Foodhallen, Vondelpark, local neighborhood feel.
Noord — Industrial-cool, A'DAM Tower, free ferry from Centraal.
Centrum — Dam Square, Red Light District, Nine Streets shopping.
Museum Quarter — Rijksmuseum, Van Gogh, upscale dining near Vondelpark.
