🏘 Neighborhood Guide▼
Jordaan
Charming canal streets, indie boutiques, cozy brown cafés, art galleries. Noordermarkt Saturdays.
De Pijp
Bohemian foodie heaven. Albert Cuyp Market is a must. Craft beer, lively atmosphere.
Museum Quarter
Rijksmuseum, Van Gogh, Stedelijk. P.C. Hooftstraat shops. Vondelpark for picnics.
Centrum
Dam Square, Red Light District, Nine Streets. Touristy but essential.
Noord
Creative frontier. Free ferry. NDSM Wharf street art, A'DAM Lookout views.
Oud-West
Foodhallen, De Hallen complex. Mid-range dining, craft cocktails.
