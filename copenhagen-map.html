🔥 Hottest Neighborhoods

Vesterbro / Kødbyen — Meatpacking District. Best nightlife + food. Coolest area.
Nørrebro — Multicultural hub. Cheap eats, hipster cafés, ethnic food.
Indre By (Center) — Strøget, fine dining, Nyhavn, historic.
Christianshavn — Canals, Christiania, waterfront vibes.
Refshaleøen — Reffen street food, industrial creative district.

🍽️ Top Restaurant Picks

Absalon — communal dinner €8! DKK 60
Høst — New Nordic, affordable DKK 395
Reffen — 50+ street food stalls DKK 60-120
Poulette — fried chicken (from The Bear!) DKK 95
Kødbyens Fiskebar — seafood in Meatpacking DKK 200+
Hija de Sanchez — tacos by ex-Noma chef DKK 50

💡 Food Tips

💰 Copenhagen Card (48h ~$95) = 80+ attractions FREE + transit
🥪 Try smørrebrød (open sandwiches) at Torvehallerne
🍺 Mikkeller brewery bars are everywhere — try one
🍽️ Absalon community dinner tickets sell out — book by 4pm
🚲 Rent bikes (~$12/day) — fastest way around the city
⚠️ CPH is expensive — budget DKK 200-400/day for food
