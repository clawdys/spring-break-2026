🚇 Transit Tips

Copenhagen Card: 80+ attractions FREE + unlimited transit · 48h = ~$95 · Pays for itself in 2 museums
M3 Cityringen (red circle): Hits everything — Nyhavn, Tivoli, Vesterbro, Nørrebro. Runs 24/7.
M1/M2 (green): Central → Frederiksberg → Vanløse · M2 goes to Airport
M4 (blue): Central → Kongens Nytorv → Nordhavn. Good for harbor area.
City is very bikeable — rent from hotel or Donkey Republic app (~$12/day)
