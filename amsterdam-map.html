🚇 Transit Tips

GVB Day Pass: €10/day — unlimited metro + tram + bus
M52 (blue): Key line. Noord ↔ Central ↔ Rokin ↔ De Pijp ↔ Zuid
Tram 2: Central → Rijksmuseum → Vondelpark
Tram 13/17: Central → Anne Frank House → Jordaan → West
Free ferry: Central Station → Noord (Sir Adam) — runs 24/7
Rent bikes from hotel (~€15/day) — fastest way around
